Emergency Veterinary Clinic

AFTER HOURS SERVICE

At Sam's all pets animal clinic, we strive to be there for you and your pet(s) in those emergency situations where you are unable to book an appointment. When we are not available and you are in need of veterinary care for your pet, call and/or vist our trusted local emergency clinic who are open during after hours, holidays and weekends. They are located in the Wexford Plaza north of Bovarid Drive, just minutes from our location Emergency Clinic Location.

Meet Our Staff

Come and meet our veterinary health team. We would love to see you and your pet!

Dr. Paul Goudas

Being in practice for over 40 years, Sam's all Pets Animal Clinic is Paul’s 3rd venture in the creation and management of a veterinary clinic. His many years of experience are only matched by his excitement and enthusiasm for this new veterinary practice.

Dr. Alice Sowers

Dr. Sowers graduated from the Ontario Veterinary College with distinction where she gained special interest in soft tissue surgery, ophthalmology, preventative medicine and pain management. Her inspiration to become a veterinarian developed when she travelled to Thailand and worked with wildlife rehabilitation. She also had the opportunity to travel to India where she volunteered in the Global Vets program gaining invaluable surgical experience while travelling abroad.
