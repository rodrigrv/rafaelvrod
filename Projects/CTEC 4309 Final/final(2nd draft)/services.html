Here's what we offer

At Sam's we offer an array of services for your pet. Take a look below!

Whether your pet needs a basic round of shots or here for emergency surgery, we've got you covered. We offer services that are second to none and and a great value. Some of our services include:

General Health Check-ups Vaccinations
Full-Service Grooming Comprehensive Surgery

Keep the population in check.

With the pet poplulation ever growing and shelters becoming overcrowded, we at Sam's offer Spay/Neutering services to make sure your pet won't add to the existing poplulation. One advantage to this service is an additional life span and also having a reduced risk of any reproductive based illness.

We also offer boarding services here at Sam's as well. With our large kennels and temperature controlled environments, we can accomodate even your biggest pets to make sure they have a nice stay after a surgery. This feature can be useful when you need to travel and can't bring your pet with you or if you want to make sure they're in good hands after a delicate surgery.

We have the best products!

While we are an animal clinic, we may as well be a store sometimes. As stated on the homepage, we offer a wide array of products ranging from food for special diets to everyday collars and leashes all at a reasonable price. Some of our products include flea & tick prevention, heartworm prevention, odor elimination products, and vitamins for most animals.

Which animals do we take?

While most clinic only accept the usual cats and dogs, we offer basic services for a variety of pets. These include, birds, rabbits/bunnies, gerbils/mice, snakes, fish, and ferrets to name a few.
