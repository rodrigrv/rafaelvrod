Looking for a fix?!

Here you might find some useful information about what's got your cat or dog or other animal going crazy.

Fleas

Nasty buggers if not taken care of. Treatments include powders, oral or topical medication.

Tapeworms

Really bad with cats. Some treatments are injection, and oral medication.

Ear Infections

Dogs hate getting these. Signs of this may be lack of balance, swelling of ear, and odor.

Hot Spots

Notorious in dogs, these red, inflammed areas can be treated by antibiotics or cleaning the area.

Heat Stroke in gerbils

Important for every animal, some symptoms for gerbils include trembling and lethargy.

What we're about!!

Get to know us by going back to our roots.

Ever since 1975 when we were first founded, Sam always had the urge to make sure that pets came first. When we first started, it was only Sam and one or two other employees. Since then, our family has grown and our love of animals have grown even more. From tending to exotic iguana's to making sure our felines get their routine nail clippings, Sam has done it all.

Where are we now?

Here at Sam's we have moved locations over the years. Starting in 1975, we started in Ft. Worth and then moved slowly to Dallas. Now for the last 5 years, we have settled here in Arlington and business couldn't be better. Slowly but surely, we have been expanding our clientele and our facility. As we are sure you've read on our home page, we now have larger kennels and another exam room. Yay for pets!!

At Sams's we strive for continued excellence with your pets and try to provide them with the best visit anyone can ask for. Ever since our start-up in 1975, we have always put in the effort your pet can know and love. Even today that still resonates with us and we hope it will show every time you come back.

Looking for us?

While it is convenient to have us anywhere you go, sadly we are only in one place. We tried to locate a place that wasn't too far from our old clients yet still close enough to gain new clients. We hope your satisfied with our choice. Our address is 319 S. Collins St Arlington, Texas 76010.
