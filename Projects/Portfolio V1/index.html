My Bio




Welcome to my portfolio page. My name is Rafael Rodriguez and I am a Communication Technology (CTEC) Major. At the moment, I'm an aspiring web developer/designer. I have around 3 years of experience creating websites. I also have experience in SEO, advertising, multimedia production and social media strategy. Previous Projects

Below are a few works I have produced since I've started on this journey. More to follow in the near future.

That Movie Place JavaJam Re-Design
This was my first ever website. Unfortunately it's not responsive but I tried to create this well with what I knew at the time. The goal of this website was to demonstrate basic HTML and CSS elements like navigation, picture links, and overall structure. I was tasked with re-designing a fictional coffee place. The goal was to implement more advanced techniques like frameworks, media queries, and video/audio capability.
How To Impress Your Friends A Day In The Life
A short clip using Premiere Pro CC, with the goal of making a quick "how to" video. I chose to do a video about a simple card trick that can be used as an icebreaker and make a new friend. A final project for my Multimedia Production class, with the goal of using various film techniques as well as properly mixing video and audio. Interesting side note: this clip was shot entirely using a Samsung Galaxy S4.
Sam's All Pet's Animal Clinic Old West Animal Hospital
This project was a collaboration with another student. The goal here was to implement a bit of functionality, advanced aesthetic appeal, and integrate SEO. It was to show our client what we could create for them since they currently don't have a website. As my latest and currently most favorite project, the goal of this website was to encompass HTML, CSS, PHP, and MySQL. It is complete with an interactive login feature and a fully working appointment system.
