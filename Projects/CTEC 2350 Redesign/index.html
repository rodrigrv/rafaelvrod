Who we are

We're a fairly new company that specializes in movies of all kinds. If you can think of a movie, odds are we have it.

Our services are second to none. Not only do we have a great physical location in your local area, but our online website is also a great way to search through our library of movies.

What we do

At our company, customer service is our top priority. With that said, we offer great services on our movies. We buy, sell, and rent every movie that comes through our store.

Renting Policy

There are a few guidelines when returning a movie. They must be returned in the condition they were given in.

  • No scratches to the disc
  • Original casing and disc must be returned
  • If disc is damaged, customer must pay for the movie in full

Selling Policy

When selling a movie, there's a few things to consider before we take a movie.

  • Customer must make sure disc is playable
  • There can be little to no scratches on the disc
  • Original casing is preferred but not required
